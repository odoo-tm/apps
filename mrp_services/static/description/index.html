This is a "proof of concept" to illustrate how service products can be used in manufacturing orders to represent direct labor costs contributing to the cost of finished goods.

This app may require changes before it is suitable to use in a production environment. Discuss your requirements with an Odoo Advisor or Odoo Partner to understand the best way to leverage this functionality.
